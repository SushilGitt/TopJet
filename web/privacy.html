Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

TopJet ("we", "our", "the app") is a Shopify application that adds a customizable scroll-to-top button to online stores. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you install and use our app.

1. Information We Collect

When you install TopJet, we collect the following information through the Shopify API:

  • Store information: Store name, store domain, email address, and Shopify plan type.
  • Session data: OAuth access tokens required to authenticate your store with our app.
  • Subscription data: Information about your current app subscription plan.

We do not collect any personal data from your customers. The scroll-to-top button operates entirely on the storefront and does not track, collect, or store any customer information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and provide the app functionality to your store.
  • To manage your subscription plan and billing through Shopify.
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.

3. Data Storage

Your session and store data is stored securely in a MongoDB database hosted on our private server. Access tokens are stored encrypted and are only used to communicate with the Shopify API on your behalf.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Your information is only used to operate the app.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as the app is installed on your store. When you uninstall the app, we delete your session data within 48 hours of receiving the shop/redact webhook from Shopify.

6. GDPR Compliance

We comply with Shopify's mandatory GDPR webhooks:

  • Customer data request: We respond to data access requests. Since we do not store customer data, no customer data is returned.
  • Customer data erasure: We process data deletion requests. Since we do not store customer data, no action is needed.
  • Shop data erasure: When a store uninstalls, we delete all associated store and session data.

7. Cookies

The app does not set any cookies on your customers' browsers. The scroll-to-top button is rendered purely via a theme app extension with no tracking scripts.

8. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Shopify API: For authentication, billing, and app functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
